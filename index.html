The modules

Thirteen tools, one workspace.

Each module is built around how road-transport actually runs in India — bilties, ferro-alloy clients, GST, weight annexures, missing-doc chases. Pick what you use, ignore what you don't.

Trip Management

Every truck movement: vehicle, vendor, route, qty, rate, freight, assigned staff. Date-range, search, 30/page.

Bilty (LR) Management

Consignment notes on your letterhead, with your stamp & signature. Mobile-friendly. PDF export ready for client + driver.

Vendor (Client) Management

Client companies, GSTIN, billing terms. Per-vendor history of trips, invoices, outstanding dues at a glance.

Invoice Generation

One click turns a month of trips into a GST tax invoice + annexure. "Transportation Charges for {goods} from {consignor} to {consignee}", totals, stamp.

Annexure Generator

Gross / Net / Received weights. Net = Gross − Received. Freight = Rate × Received. Totals computed. PDF.

Expense Tracking

Fuel, tolls, driver advances per trip. Freight auto-flows into Reports & Analytics — no double entry.

Billing Reconciliation

Vendor-wise billed vs paid. Invoice-aware totals. Know exactly who owes what, today.

Missing Bilty Tracker

Flags trips without an uploaded bilty so nothing leaks into next month's billing. Goal: stay at zero.

Driver Upload Portal

Drivers upload PODs/bilties from a phone — no admin login. Documents land in the right trip instantly.

Reports & Analytics

Freight totals, expense breakdowns, vendor-wise summaries. Today, this week, this month — built-in date ranges.

Notifications (ntfy)

Admin / manager broadcasts to staff or drivers. Auto-pings on new invoice, trip, bilty, vendor.

Role-Based Access

Admin · Manager · Staff (per-module) · Driver · Super Admin. Staff only sees what's theirs.

Live Multi-User Sync

Every change shows everywhere instantly. Two staff entering trips? Both see each other live. No refresh.

How teams actually use it

From the dispatch desk to the owner's phone.

Month-end billing

A day of invoicing becomes a minute.

"Generate invoice for Sharp Ferro Alloys, May 2026" → tax invoice + weight annexure built from the month's trips, stamped, ready to send. No copy-pasting from Tally, no double entry.

Daily dispatch

Trucks leave, the screen knows.

Dispatcher logs trips as vehicles depart; staff updates as bilties arrive; the Missing Bilty Tracker stays at zero by Friday. Nothing slips into next month's reconciliation.

Driver docs on the road

POD photo → right trip, instantly.

Driver opens the driver portal, snaps the bilty or POD, picks the trip. No login admin, no WhatsApp scroll, no "which group was that in?"

Payment chasing

Owner opens one tab. Sees who owes what.

Billing Reconciliation shows vendor-wise billed vs paid, invoice-aware. The follow-up list writes itself.
